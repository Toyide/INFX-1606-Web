INFX 1606 Finally Holds First Lab

The course, Introduction to Website Creation (INFX 1606), finally held its first lab of the Fall 2015 term on Thursday, Sep 24, 2015. After studying several aspects of website design, students finally get to apply their knowledge in their labs.
